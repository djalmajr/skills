# Persona — Power user

An experienced, frequent user (often internal/back-office) who values **speed and control** over hand-holding. The lens for efficiency and depth.

## Profile

- Knows the domain and the app; does the same tasks many times a day.
- Wants density, shortcuts, bulk actions, and to skip confirmations they understand.
- Annoyed by wizards, forced steps, and UI that hides power behind too many clicks.

## How this persona judges (lenses)

- "**Is there a faster way?**" — keyboard shortcuts, defaults, recently-used, bulk operations.
- "**Don't nanny me.**" — repeated obvious confirmations and forced tutorials waste time.
- "**Show me everything.**" — values information density and at-a-glance status over whitespace.
- "**Let me recover fast.**" — quick undo/edit beats blocking guardrails for an expert.

## Typical phrases (first person)

- "I do this fifty times a day — there has to be a shortcut."
- "Why am I clicking through three screens for a one-field change?"
- "Let me select them all and act once, not one by one."

# Persona — Skeptical

A cautious user who is **wary of giving data** and distrusts anything that feels off. The lens for trust, transparency, and privacy.

## Profile

- Reads before clicking; suspicious of forms that ask for more than seems necessary.
- Worries about where their data goes and whether an action is reversible.
- Looks for reassurance: why is this asked, who sees it, can I undo it.

## How this persona judges (lenses)

- "**Why do you need this?**" — every field should justify itself; over-asking erodes trust.
- "**Is this safe?**" — sensitive actions need clear, calm framing (not dark patterns).
- "**Can I undo it?**" — irreversible actions must be explicit and confirmable.
- "**Who sees this?**" — visibility/consent of submitted data should be stated.

## Typical phrases (first person)

- "Why does it need my full document number just to look something up?"
- "Is this going to be public? It doesn't say."
- "There's no 'cancel' — what if I made a mistake?"

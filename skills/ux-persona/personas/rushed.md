# Persona — Rushed

Someone who knows exactly what they want and wants the **shortest path**. The lens for measuring friction and step count.

## Profile

- Has a clear goal and little time; came to do one thing only.
- Skips long text; scans the screen for the button that resolves the task.
- Bails at the first redundant step, giant form, or wait without feedback.

## How this persona judges (lenses)

- "**What's the shortcut?**" — counts clicks to the goal; every extra step costs.
- "**Do I really need this?**" — optional fields disguised as required ones are infuriating.
- "**Why so slow?**" — with no progress/loading indicator, assumes it's stuck.
- "**Done yet?**" — wants the result confirmation immediate and unambiguous.

## Typical phrases (first person)

- "I just want <goal>, I don't want to read a manual."
- "Again? Didn't I already fill this in?"
- "Where's the button? I'm about to give up."
